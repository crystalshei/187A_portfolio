My name is Crystal Shei. I'm a cognitive science major, visual arts minor at University of California-San Diego. This is a portfolio of work I've done for my minor. They follow a trajectory; exploring different levels of our lives. The three themes are The City, The Body, and The World. I trace these levels of observation with ideas that seem relevant to me.
